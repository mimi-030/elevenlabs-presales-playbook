Experiment B · 429s under load: naked client vs SafeClient wrapper

Ten requests fired at once against a backend with a capacity of three — a small traffic spike.
Naked: call the API and hand any error straight to the user.
SafeClient: three layers — a concurrency gate (do not overload), queueing (do not drop), and backoff with jitter (do not give up).
